Book Recommender System



Description


Instacart is a grocery delivery application that works in the following way- users can select products through the app, and then personal shoppers review the order, shop in-store and deliver the products to the users. We got the data from Instacart's Market Basket Analysis on kaggle.com here. The Instacart data set is anonymized and contains samples of over 3 million grocery orders from 200,000+ Instacart users. On its website Instacart has a recommendation system that suggests the users some items to buy again. Our task is to predict which items will be reordered on the next order. We approach this using 3 methods:
-Logistic Regression: to predict product reorder
-Association Rule Mining: utilizing the apriori algorithm and perform market basket analysis to generate association rules with different values of support and confidence, to predict what products are closely associated with or frequently bought with other products
-Gradient Boosting Model: to predict product reorders as well as produce association rules within the test dataset.
